## Additional Context: Exodus 34:27 and the Writing Distinction

> **Exodus 34:27** And the LORD said unto Moses, Write thou these words: for after the tenor of these words I have made a covenant with thee and with Israel.

Note: This verse commands Moses to write "these words" (the preceding instructions in 34:11-26), while v.28 states "he wrote upon the tables the words of the covenant, the ten commandments." The "he" in v.28 who wrote on the tables is identified by context: Moses wrote in v.27 (the book), God wrote on the tables (as stated in 34:1, "I will write").
