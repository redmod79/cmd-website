## Key Study Conclusion Summaries

### law-27 (Sabbath Still in Effect -- Capstone)
"The biblical evidence establishes that the seventh-day Sabbath remains binding. No verse in the New Testament explicitly abolishes the weekly Sabbath or transfers its sanctity to another day; instead, the Sabbath is grounded in creation (Gen 2:2-3), confirmed by Jesus as Lord of the Sabbath (Mrk 2:28), treated as operative after the crucifixion by Luke (Luk 23:56), anticipated by Jesus for the post-cross era (Mat 24:20), declared presently remaining by Hebrews using the practice-word sabbatismos (Heb 4:9), and prophesied to continue into the new earth (Isa 66:22-23)."

E+N tally: 219 Continues, 0 Abolished (law-31 synthesis). All abolition arguments exist at I-tier only.

### law-25 (Sabbath Moral or Ceremonial)
Seven categories of evidence: (1) creation origin, (2) Decalogue membership with all 7 unique markers, (3) delivery mode (God's voice + finger), (4) Lev 23:37-38 separation, (5) zakar ("remember") pointing backward vs. skia ("shadow") pointing forward, (6) Heb 4:9 sabbatismos, (7) universal scope (anthropos, Isa 56, Isa 66). "Across all seven criteria, the weekly Sabbath aligns with every marker of the moral law and none of the markers of the ceremonial law."

### law-24 (Weekly vs. Ceremonial Sabbaths)
"The Bible itself distinguishes the weekly Sabbath from the annual ceremonial sabbaths through: structural separation in Lev 23, vocabulary separation in Lev 23:37-38, two distinct work-prohibition levels (kol-melakhah vs. melekhet abodah), distinct offering categories in Num 28-29." The weekly Sabbath is never called moed or chag.

### law-13 (Jesus and the Sabbath)
"Every recorded Sabbath controversy shows Jesus arguing WITHIN the Sabbath's legal framework (using exesti). He defines lawful Sabbath activity, declares healing morally obligatory (edei), and as 'Lord of the Sabbath' exercises governing authority by defining the Sabbath's true requirements, not by abolishing it."

Key anthropos finding: "anthropos (G444) is GENERIC humanity, not a specific ethnic group. Not Ioudaios (Jew), not Israelites, but anthropos -- humanity."
