- **Pronunciation:** naw-af

## [H2181](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h2181/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} -- זָנָה (zanah)
- **Transliteration:** zanah
- **Pronunciation:** zaw-naw
- **Part of Speech:** verb
- **Occurrences:** 93
- **Definition:** A primitive root [highly fed and therefore wanton]; to commit adultery (usually of the female, and less often of simple fornication, rarely of involuntary ravishment); figuratively, to commit idolatry
- **Translations:** commit fornication, continually, great, harlot, whore, whoredom, go a-whoring, whorish
- **Key verses:** Gen 34:31; 38:15,24; Exo 34:15-16; Lev 17:7; 19:29; 20:5-6; Num 5:19; 25:1; Deu 22:21; 23:19; 31:16; Jos 2:1; 6:17,22,25; Jdg 2:17; 8:27,33; 11:1; 16:1; 19:2; many prophetic passages (Isa, Jer, Ezk, Hos)
- **Note:** Broader than [H5003](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h5003/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} -- covers harlotry, fornication, and figurative idolatry

- **Pronunciation:** baw-gad

## [G1937](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1937/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} -- ἐπιθυμέω (epithumeo)
- **Transliteration:** epithumeo
- **Pronunciation:** ep-ee-thoo-meh-o
- **Part of Speech:** verb
- **Occurrences:** 16
- **Definition:** To set the heart upon, i.e. long for (rightfully or otherwise)
- **Key verses:** Exo 20:17 (2x); Deu 5:21; Mat 5:28 (2x); 13:17; Luk 15:16; 16:21; 17:22; 22:15; Acts 20:33; Rom 7:7; 13:9; 1 Cor 10:6; Gal 5:17; 1 Tim 3:1; Heb 6:11; Jas 4:2; 1 Pet 1:12; Rev 9:6
- **Note:** Not inherently negative -- can mean righteous desire (Luk 22:15; 1 Tim 3:1; Heb 6:11) or sinful lust (Mat 5:28; Rom 7:7). In Mat 5:28, the context (πρὸς τὸ ἐπιθυμῆσαι = "for the purpose of lusting") makes clear the sinful intent.
