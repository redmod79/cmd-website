## Greek — Defrauding

### [G650](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g650/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — ἀποστερέω (apostereo) — verb
- Transliteration: aposteréō
- Part of Speech: verb
- BLB Count: 6 occurrences
- Definition: from apo and stereo (to deprive); to despoil: defraud, destitute
- Key verses: Mrk 10:19; 1 Cor 6:7,8; 7:5; 1 Tim 6:5; Jas 5:4
- Also found in LXX: Deu 24:14; Neh 9:20; Mal 3:5
- Translation: defraud, destitute, be defrauded
- NOTE: Jesus uses this word in Mrk 10:19 ("Defraud not") — an addition to the standard commandment list not found in Matthew/Luke parallels
