# Topics Research

## Question
What does the Bible say about the ninth commandment? What does "false witness" (ed shaqer / ed shav) mean -- Exodus uses sheqer (H8267, "lying/deceitful") while Deuteronomy uses shav (H7723, "vain/worthless"). Is this limited to courtroom testimony or does it cover all falsehood?
