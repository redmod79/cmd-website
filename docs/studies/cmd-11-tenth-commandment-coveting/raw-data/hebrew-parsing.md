**Key finding:** Deuteronomy 5:21 uses TWO DIFFERENT VERBS:
1. תַחְמֹ֖ד (tachmad) — chamad (H2530), Qal Impf 2ms — for "wife" (אשׁת)
2. תִתְאַוֶּ֜ה (titavveh) — avah (H183), Hithpael Impf 2ms — for "house" (בית) and all property

**Differences from Exodus 20:17:**
- Order reversed: wife first, then house (Exodus has house first, then wife)
- Two verbs instead of one
- "field" (שׂדה) added to the list (not in Exodus)
- Setumah (ס) paragraph marker between the two clauses in Deu 5:21
