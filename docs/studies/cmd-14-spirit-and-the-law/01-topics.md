# Topics Research

## Question
What does the Bible say about the Holy Spirit's role in enabling commandment-keeping? Trace from Old Testament promise (Eze 36:26-27) through New Testament fulfillment (Rom 8:1-14; Gal 5:16-25; 2 Cor 3:3-18): How does the Spirit write the law on hearts, produce the fruit that satisfies the law's demands, and empower believers to "walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit"?
