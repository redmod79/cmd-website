# Topics Research

## Question
What commandments is Jesus referring to in John 14:15? How does "If ye love me, keep my commandments" parallel Exodus 20:6's "them that love me, and keep my commandments"? Trace the love-commandments formula from Exodus 20:6 through every biblical occurrence. Analyze the Greek grammar of John 14:15. Examine who was speaking in Exodus 20. How do John's epistles define the love-commandment relationship? What is the significance of Revelation 14:12?

## Related Topics Discovered

### SPIRITUALITY (from Nave's search for DEVOTION)
- **Defined as love and devotion to God:** DEU 6:5; JOS 22:5; 1KI 8:23; PSA 1:2; 51:6
- **Is produced by the indwelling of the Holy Spirit:** JHN 14:16,17; ROM 8:4

This connects directly to John 14:15-17: the commandment to keep commandments is immediately followed by the promise of the Holy Spirit (the Comforter), who produces the spiritual love that enables obedience.
