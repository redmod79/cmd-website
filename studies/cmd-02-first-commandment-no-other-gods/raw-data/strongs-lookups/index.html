    Raw Strong's Lookup Data¶

    H430 -- elohim¶

    • 2,685 total occurrences, 165 unique translations
    • Top: God (734, 27.3%), thy God (323, 12.0%), of God (321, 12.0%), gods (103, 3.8%), the judges (4, 0.1%)
    • Lexicon: plural of H433; gods in the ordinary sense; but specifically used (in the plural, especially with the article) of the supreme God; occasionally applied by way of deference to magistrates; and sometimes as a superlative

    H433 -- eloah¶

    • 62 total occurrences, 17 unique translations
    • Top: God (33, 53.2%), of God (9, 14.5%), god (3, 4.8%)
    • Lexicon: probably prolonged (emphatic) from H410; a deity or the Deity

    H410 -- el¶

    • 271 total occurrences, 55 unique translations
    • Top: God (130, 48.0%), of God (16, 5.9%), god (7, 2.6%), in the power (3, 1.1%)
    • Lexicon: shortened from H352; strength; as adjective, mighty; especially the Almighty (but used also of any deity)

    H6440 -- panim¶

    • 2,141 total occurrences, 295 unique translations
    • Top: before (683, 31.9%), before thee (99, 4.6%), before him (94, 4.4%), the face (77, 3.6%), before me (71, 3.3%), his face (53, 2.5%), my face (50, 2.3%)
    • Lexicon: plural (but always as singular) from H6437; the face (as the part that turns); used in a great variety of applications

    H259 -- echad¶

    • 973 total occurrences, 98 unique translations
    • Top: one (509, 52.3%), a (55, 5.7%), first (14, 1.4%), together (5, 0.5%), alone (4, 0.4%)
    • Lexicon: a numeral from H258; properly, united, i.e. one; or (as an ordinal) first

    H7067 -- qanna¶

    • 6 total occurrences, 3 unique translations (ALL "jealous")
    • Only used of God: Exo 20:5; 34:14 (2x); Deu 4:24; 5:9; 6:15
    • Lexicon: from H7065; jealous

    H7072 -- qanno¶

    • 2 total occurrences, 1 unique translation ("jealous")
    • Only used of God: Jos 24:19; Nah 1:2 (BLB also shows Deu 6:15)
    • Lexicon: for H7067; jealous or angry

    H7068 -- qin'ah¶

    • 45 total occurrences, 28 unique translations
    • Top: of jealousy (6, 13.3%), envy (4, 8.9%), jealousy (4, 8.9%), the zeal (3, 6.7%)
    • Used of both divine jealousy and human envy/zeal
    • Lexicon: from H7065; jealousy or envy

    H457 -- eliyl¶

    • 21 total occurrences, 11 unique translations
    • Top: idols (9, 42.9%), of idols (2, 9.5%), the idols (2, 9.5%)
    • Lexicon: apparently from H408; good for nothing, by analogy vain or vanity; specifically an idol
    • Wordplay on el (H410) -- the "mighty ones" are actually "worthless nothings"

    H8655 -- teraphim¶

    • 15 total occurrences, 8 unique translations
    • Top: the images (4, 26.7%), the teraphim (3, 20.0%), an image (2, 13.3%)
    • Lexicon: plural from H7495; a healer; Teraphim (singular or plural) a family idol
    • Key: Gen 31:19; Jdg 17:5; 1Sa 15:23; Hos 3:4

    G2316 -- theos¶

    • 1,151 total occurrences, 37 unique translations
    • Top: God (560, 48.7%), of God (461, 40.1%), to God (38, 3.3%), gods (6, 0.5%)
    • Lexicon: of uncertain affinity; a deity, especially (with the article) the supreme Divinity

    G1497 -- eidolon¶

    • 10 total occurrences, 4 unique translations
    • Top: idols (6, 60.0%), idol (2, 20.0%)
    • Lexicon: from G1491; an image (i.e. for worship); by implication, a heathen god

    G1495 -- eidololatreia¶

    • 4 total occurrences: 1Co 10:14; Gal 5:20; Col 3:5; 1Pe 4:3
    • Lexicon: from G1497 and G2999; image-worship

    G1496 -- eidololatres¶

    • 7 total occurrences: 1Co 5:10-11; 6:9; 10:7; Eph 5:5; Rev 21:8; 22:15
    • Lexicon: from G1497 and the base of G3000; an image-worshipper

    G2320 -- theotes¶

    • 1 total occurrence: Col 2:9
    • Lexicon: from G2316; divinity (abstractly)

    G2304 -- theios¶

    • 3 total occurrences: Acts 17:29; 2Pe 1:3; 1:4
    • Lexicon: from G2316; godlike (neuter as noun, divinity)
