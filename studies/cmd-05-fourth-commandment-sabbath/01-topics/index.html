    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    What does the Bible say about the Sabbath commandment? This study builds on the law series (7 existing Sabbath studies) and focuses on what has NOT been fully covered: the commandment's creation basis (Gen 2:2-3), its explicit extension to non-Israelites (Exo 20:10 -- "the stranger within thy gates"), Isaiah's invitation to foreigners (Isa 56:6-7), and Jesus's statement that "the sabbath was made for man" (Mark 2:27 -- anthropos, generic humanity).

    Concept Context for Exo 20:8¶

    The concept analysis for Exo 20:8 identified the concept HOLY (qadash/hagios) as the primary theological concept in this verse. Key connected passages include: - Same chapter: Exo 20:11 (hallowed the Sabbath) - Same book: Exo 12:16 (holy convocation, seventh day, no work); Exo 16:23 (holy sabbath unto the LORD); Exo 19:10,14,22,23 (sanctification) - Same author (Moses): Deu 5:12 (keep the sabbath day to sanctify it); Deu 14:2 (holy people); Lev 19:30 (keep my sabbaths)
