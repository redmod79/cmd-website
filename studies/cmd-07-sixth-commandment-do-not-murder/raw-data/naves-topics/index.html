    Raw Nave's Topic Data — cmd-07¶

    HOMICIDE¶

    -ACCIDENTAL EXO 21:13,28-32; NUM 35:11-15,22-28,32; DEU 4:41-43; 19:2-10; JOS 20:1-9
-FELONIOUS, OR MURDER GEN 4:9-12; 9:5,6; 49:7; EXO 20:13; 21:29-32; NUM 35:16-22,30,31; DEU 5:17; 17:6; 21:1-9; 22:8; 27:24,25; 1KI 21:19; 2CH 24:22; JOB 24:1-25; PSA 5:6; 9:12; 10:2; 26:9,10; 37:32; 38:12; 55:23; 94:3; PRO 1:11,12,15,16; 6:16,17; 12:6; 28:17; ISA 26:21; 59:3,7; JER 2:34; 7:9,10; 19:4; 22:3; EZK 22:9; 35:6; HOS 1:4; 4:1-3; HAB 2:10,12; MAT 5:21,22; 15:19; 19:18; MRK 7:21; 10:19; LUK 18:20; ROM 13:9; GAL 5:19-21; 1TI 1:9; JAS 2:11; 1PE 4:15; 1JN 3:12,15; REV 9:21; 21:8; 22:15
-DAVID'S REPENTANCE FOR, AND CONFESSION OF, THE MURDER OF URIAH PSA 51:1-17
-INSTANCES OF FELONIOUS (numerous instances listed)
-PUNISHMENT OF GEN 4:13-15; 9:5,6; 27:43-45; EXO 21:12,14; LEV 24:17; NUM 35:16-21,30-33; DEU 19:11-13; 2SA 12:9-12

    HATRED¶

    -Against iniquity, justified PSA 97:10; 101:3; 119:104,128,163; 139:21,22
-Of God PSA 5:5; 45:7; MAL 2:16
-SCRIPTURES RELATING TO LEV 19:17; PSA 25:19; 35:19; PRO 10:12,18; 15:17; 26:24-26; MAT 5:43,44; 6:15; 10:22; JHN 15:18,19,23-25; 17:14; GAL 5:19,20; EPH 4:31; COL 3:8; 1JN 2:9,11; 3:10,13-15; 4:20

    ANGER¶

    -General scriptures concerning GEN 4:6; 49:7; 2CH 28:9; JOB 5:2; 19:29; PSA 37:8; 55:3; 76:10; PRO 6:34; 12:16; 14:17,29; 15:1,18; 16:14,29,32; 17:14; 19:11,12,19; 21:24; 22:24,25; 25:28; 27:3,4; 29:8,9,22; 30:33; ECC 7:9; HOS 7:16; AMO 1:11; JON 4:4; MAT 5:22; 2CO 12:20; GAL 5:19-21; EPH 4:26,31; COL 3:8; 1TI 2:8; TIT 1:7; JAS 1:19,20
-INSTANCES OF: Cain (GEN 4:5-8), Simeon/Levi (GEN 49:5-7), and many others

    PUNISHMENT (Capital)¶

    -DEATH PENALTY — Will not be commuted NUM 35:31
-For murder GEN 9:5,6; NUM 35:16-21,30-33; DEU 17:6
-MODES: Burning, Stoning, Hanging, Beheading, Crucifixion, Sword
-Not inflicted by testimony of less than two witnesses NUM 35:30; DEU 17:6; 19:15

    ASSASSINATION¶

    -David's abhorrence of 2SA 4:9-12
-Laws prohibiting DEU 27:24
-INSTANCES OF: Eglon by Ehud, Abner by Joab, Ish-bosheth by sons of Rimmon, Amnon by Absalom, Amasa by Joab, Joash by servants, Sennacherib by sons

    MASSACRE¶

    -Authorized by Moses DEU 20:13,16
-Decree to destroy the Jews EST 3
-INSTANCES OF: Inhabitants of Heshbon, Bashan, Ai, Hazor, etc.

    REFUGE (Cities of)¶

    -From the avenger of blood EXO 21:13,14; NUM 35:11-32; DEU 4:41-43; 19:2-13; JOS 20:1-6
-List of JOS 20:7-9
-Roads made to DEU 19:3
-FIGURATIVE: Of Christ HEB 6:18

    BLOOD¶

    -Is the life GEN 9:4; LEV 17:11,14; 19:16; DEU 12:23; MAT 27:4,24
-Forbidden to be used as food GEN 9:4; LEV 3:17; 7:26,27; 17:10-14
-FIGURATIVE: Of victories PSA 58:10; Of oppression HAB 2:12; Of destruction EZK 35:6; Of guilt LEV 20:9; 2SA 1:16; EZK 18:13

    MALICE¶

    -General scriptures concerning GEN 3:15; LEV 19:14,17,18; DEU 27:17,18; JOB 15:35; PSA 4:2; 7:14-16; MAT 5:38-41; JHN 8:44; ROM 1:29-32; 1JN 2:9,11; 3:10,13-15; 4:20
-INSTANCES OF: Cain toward Abel, Esau toward Jacob, Joseph's brothers, etc.

    RETALIATION¶

    -General scriptures concerning EXO 21:23-25; LEV 19:18; 24:17-22; DEU 19:19-21; MAT 5:38-44; ROM 12:17,19; 1PE 3:9

    STRIFE¶

    -General scriptures concerning GEN 13:8; PRO 6:12-14,16-19; 10:12; 15:18; 17:14,19; GAL 5:19-21; JAS 4:1,2

    COMMANDMENTS (Ten)¶

    -General scriptures EXO 13:8-10; 20:3-17; DEU 4:5,9,10; 5:6-21
-PRECEPTS OF JESUS MAT 5:16,22-24,27-48; etc.
-See HOMICIDE cross-reference
