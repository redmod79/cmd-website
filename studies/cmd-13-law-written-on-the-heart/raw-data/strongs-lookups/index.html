    Raw Strong's Concordance Lookups¶

    Hebrew Strong's Numbers¶

    H1285: בְּרִית (beriyth) -- covenant¶

    • Transliteration: bᵉrîyth
    • Pronunciation: ber-eeth
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 284
    • Definition: from (in the sense of cutting [like]); a compact (because made by passing between pieces of flesh): confederacy, (con-)feder(-ate), covenant, league.
    • Key occurrences: Gen 6:18; 9:9-17; 15:18; 17:1-21; Exo 2:24; 19:5; 24:7-8; 31:16; 34:10,27-28; Deu 4:13,23,31; 5:2-3; 7:9,12; 9:9,11,15; 29:1,8,11; 31:16; Jer 11:2-10; 22:9; 31:31-33; 32:40; 34:8-18; 50:5; Eze 16:8,59-62; 17:13-19; 34:25; 37:26; Dan 9:4,27; Hos 6:7; 8:1; Mal 2:4-14; 3:1
    • LXX mapping: G1242 διαθήκη (count: 246, PMI: 6.28, score: 34.58) -- primary equivalent; G1303 διατίθεμαι (count: 67, PMI: 6.21, score: 26.22)

    H3789: כָּתַב (kathab) -- write¶

    • Transliteration: kâthab
    • Pronunciation: kaw-thab
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 223
    • Definition: a primitive root; to grave, by implication, to write (describe, inscribe, prescribe, subscribe): describe, record, prescribe, subscribe, write(-ing, -ten).
    • Key occurrences: Exo 24:4,12; 31:18; 32:15,32; 34:1,27; Deu 6:9; 9:10; 10:4; 11:20; 17:18; 27:3; 31:9,24; Jos 1:8; 8:32; Psa 40:7-8; 102:19; Pro 3:3; 7:3; Isa 8:1; 30:8; Jer 17:1; 31:33; 36:2,28; Eze 2:10; 37:16; Dan 12:1; Hos 8:12; Mal 3:16
    • LXX mapping: G1125 γράφω (count: 185, PMI: 6.63, score: 34.64) -- primary equivalent; also G1924 ἐπιγράφω (count: 4, PMI: 6.75, score: 10.87); G4109 πλάξ (count: 9, PMI: 5.47, score: 12.58)
    • Verses translated "written": Exo 31:18; 32:15; Deu 9:10; 28:61; Psa 40:7; 149:9; Jer 17:1; Eze 2:10; Dan 9:11,13; 12:1 (+ 78 more in historical/chronicle contexts)

    H8451: תּוֹרָה (torah) -- law/instruction¶

    • Transliteration: tôwrâh
    • Pronunciation: to-raw
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 219
    • Definition: from; a precept or statute, especially the Decalogue or Pentateuch: law.
    • Key occurrences: Gen 26:5; Exo 12:49; 13:9; 16:4,28; 18:16,20; 24:12; Deu 1:5; 4:8,44; 17:11,18-19; 27:3,8,26; 28:58,61; 29:20,28; 30:10; 31:9-26; 33:4,10; Jos 1:7-8; 8:31-34; 22:5; 23:6; Psa 1:2; 19:8; 37:31; 40:9; 78:1,5,10; 89:31; 94:12; 105:45; 119:1,18,29,34,44,51,53,55,61,70,72,77,85,92,97,109,126,136,142,150,153,163,165,174; Pro 1:8; 3:1; 4:2; 6:20,23; 7:2; 13:14; 28:4,7,9; 29:18; 31:26; Isa 1:10; 2:3; 5:24; 8:16,20; 42:4,21,24; 51:4,7; Jer 2:8; 6:19; 8:8; 9:12; 16:11; 18:18; 26:4; 31:33; 32:23; 44:10,23; Eze 7:26; 22:26; 43:11-12; 44:5,24; Dan 9:10-11,13; Hos 4:6; 8:1,12; Mal 2:6-9
    • LXX mapping: G3551 νόμος (count: 188, PMI: 6.45, score: 33.81) -- primary equivalent; also G1125 γράφω (count: 32), G1785 ἐντολή (count: 23), G5442 φυλάσσω (count: 32)

    H3820: לֵב (leb) -- heart¶

    • Transliteration: lêb
    • Pronunciation: labe
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun (n-m)
    • BLB Count: 593
    • Definition: a form of; the heart; also used (figuratively) very widely for the feelings, the will and even the intellect; likewise for the centre of anything: care for, comfortably, consent, considered, courag(-eous), friend(-ly), ((broken-), (hard-), (merry-), (stiff-), (stout-), double) heart(-ed), heed, I, kindly, midst, mind(-ed), regard(-ed), themselves, unawares, understanding, well, willingly, wisdom.
    • Key occurrences: Gen 6:5; 8:21; Exo 4:14,21; 7:3,13-14; 25:2; 28:3,29-30; 31:6; 35:5,10,21-26; 36:1-2; Deu 28:65; 29:3-4; Pro 2:2,10; 3:3,5; 6:18,21; 7:3; Psa 4:8; 7:10; 19:9; 37:4; 51:12,19; 119:2,10,34,36,58,69,111-112; Jer 3:10,15-17; 4:19; 5:21; 7:24; 11:8,20; 12:3; 17:1,10; 24:7; 31:33; 32:35; Eze 2:4; 3:7; 11:19; 13:2,17; 14:3-7; 18:21; 36:26; 44:5,7,9
    • LXX mapping: G2588 καρδία (count: 436, PMI: 4.80, score: 29.17) -- primary equivalent; G1271 διάνοια (count: 20, PMI: 4.46, score: 13.58)

    H3824: לֵבָב (lebab) -- heart¶

    • Transliteration: lêbâb
    • Pronunciation: lay-bawb
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun (n-m)
    • BLB Count: 252
    • Definition: from; used also like the heart (as the most interior organ): bethink themselves, breast, comfortably, courage, (faint), (tender-)heart(-ed), midst, mind, unawares, understanding.
    • Key occurrences: Deu 4:9,29,39; 5:26; 6:5-6; 8:2,5,14,17; 9:4-5; 10:12,16; 11:13,16,18; 13:4; 15:7,9-10; 17:17,20; 26:16; 28:28,47,67; 29:17-18; 30:1-2,6,10,14,17; 32:46; Jos 22:5; 23:14; 24:23; 1 Sa 7:3; 12:20,24; 13:14; 16:7; 1 Ki 2:4; 3:6; 8:17-18,38-39,47-48,58,61; 9:4; 2 Ki 23:3,25; 1 Ch 22:19; 28:9; 29:17-19; 2 Ch 6:7-8,30,37-38; Psa 86:11-12; 101:2,4-5; 119:7; Jer 4:4; 29:13; 32:40-41

    H7130: קֶרֶב (qereb) -- inward part/midst¶

    • Transliteration: qereb
    • Pronunciation: keh-reb
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun (n-m)
    • BLB Count: 227
    • Definition: from; properly, the nearest part, i.e. the center, whether literal, figurative or adverbial (especially with preposition): among, before, bowels, unto charge, eat (up), heart, him, in, inward (-ly, part, -s, thought), midst, out of, purtenance, therein, through, within self.
    • Key occurrences: Gen 25:22; 41:21; Exo 29:13; Psa 5:10; 36:2; 39:4; 48:10; 49:12; 51:12; 55:5,16; 62:5; 64:7; 74:4,11; 94:19; 101:2,7; 103:1; 109:18,22; Pro 14:33; 26:24; Isa 16:11; 19:1,3,14; Jer 4:14; 9:7; 23:9; 31:33; Eze 11:19; 36:26-27

    H7307: רוּחַ (ruach) -- spirit/wind/breath¶

    • Transliteration: rûwach
    • Pronunciation: roo-akh
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 378
    • Definition: from; wind; by resemblance breath, i.e. a sensible (or even violent) exhalation; figuratively, life, anger, unsubstantiality; by extension, a region of the sky; by resemblance spirit, but only of a rational being (including its expression and functions): air, anger, blast, breath, cool, courage, mind, quarter, side, spirit(-ual), tempest, vain, (whirl-)wind(-y).
    • Key occurrences: Gen 1:2; 6:3; 41:38; Exo 31:3; 35:31; Num 11:17,25-26,29; 14:24; 24:2; 27:18; Deu 34:9; Jdg 3:10; 6:34; 11:29; 14:6,19; 1 Sa 10:6,10; 11:6; 16:13-14; 2 Sa 23:2; Isa 11:2; 32:15; 42:1; 44:3; 48:16; 59:21; 61:1; 63:10-11; Eze 11:5,19,24; 18:31; 36:26-27; 37:1,5-6,8-10,14; 39:29; Joel 2:28-29; Zec 4:6; 12:10

    H2319: חָדָשׁ (chadash) -- new¶

    • Transliteration: châdâsh
    • Pronunciation: khaw-dawsh
    • Part of Speech: adjective (adj)
    • BLB Count: 53
    • Definition: from; new: fresh, new thing.
    • Key occurrences: Exo 1:8; Deu 32:17; Jdg 5:8; Psa 33:3; 40:4; 96:1; 98:1; 144:9; 149:1; Isa 42:9-10; 43:19; 48:6; 62:2; 65:17; 66:22; Jer 31:22,31; Lam 3:23; Eze 11:19; 18:31; 36:26

    Greek Strong's Numbers¶

    G1242: διαθήκη (diatheke) -- covenant/testament¶

    • Transliteration: diathḗkē
    • Pronunciation: dee-ath-ay-kay
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 33
    • Definition: from; properly, a disposition, i.e. (specially) a contract
    • Key occurrences: Mat 26:28; Mrk 14:24; Luk 1:72; 22:20; Acts 3:25; 7:8; Rom 9:4; 11:27; 1 Cor 11:25; 2 Cor 3:6,14; Gal 3:15,17; 4:24; Eph 2:12; Heb 7:22; 8:6,8-10; 9:4,15-16,18,20; 10:16,29; 12:24; 13:20; Rev 11:19

    G3551: νόμος (nomos) -- law¶

    • Transliteration: nómos
    • Pronunciation: nom-os
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun (n-m)
    • BLB Count: 197
    • Definition: from a primary νέμω (to parcel out, especially food or grazing to animals)
    • Key occurrences: Mat 5:17-18; 7:12; 22:36,40; Luk 2:22-27; 10:26; 16:17; Jhn 1:17,45; 7:19,49,51; 10:34; Rom 2:12-27; 3:19-21,27,31; 4:14-16; 5:13-14; 6:14; 7:1-25; 8:2-4,7; 9:31; 10:4-5; 13:8,10; 1 Cor 9:8-9,20; 14:21,34,37; 15:56; Gal 2:19,21; 3:10-13,17-19,23-24; 4:4,21; 5:3-4,14,18,23; 6:2,13; Eph 2:15; Heb 7:5,12,16,19,28; 8:10; 9:22; 10:1,16,28; Jas 1:25; 2:8,10,12

    G1449: ἐγγράφω (engrapho) -- inscribe/engrave¶

    • Transliteration: engráphō
    • Pronunciation: eng-graf-o
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 2
    • Definition: from and; to "engrave", i.e. inscribe: write (in).
    • Occurrences: Luk 10:20; 2 Cor 3:2; 3:3
    • Verses translated "written": 2 Cor 3:2; 3:3

    G1924: ἐπιγράφω (epigrapho) -- write upon/inscribe¶

    • Transliteration: epigráphō
    • Pronunciation: ep-ee-graf-o
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 5
    • Definition: from and; to inscribe (physically or mentally)
    • Occurrences: Pro 7:3 (LXX); Mrk 15:26; Luk 23:38 (v.l.); Acts 17:23; Heb 8:10; 10:16; Rev 21:12
    • Verses translated "write": Heb 8:10

    G1125: γράφω (grapho) -- write/grave¶

    • Transliteration: gráphō
    • Pronunciation: graf-o
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 209
    • Definition: a primary verb; to "grave", especially to write; figuratively, to describe
    • Key occurrences: Mat 4:4,6,10; 26:24; Mrk 1:2; 7:6; Luk 1:3,63; 4:8,10; 10:20; Jhn 1:45; 5:46; 8:6; 19:19-22; Acts 15:23; Rom 1:17; 2:24; 3:4; 4:24; 10:5; 12:19; 1 Cor 1:31; 2:9; 5:9,11; 9:10; 10:11; 14:37; 2 Cor 1:13; 2:3-4,9; Rev 1:3,11,19; 2:1,8,12,17-18; 3:1,7,12,14; 5:1; 14:1,13; 19:16; 21:27

    G2588: καρδία (kardia) -- heart¶

    • Transliteration: kardía
    • Pronunciation: kar-dee-ah
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 160
    • Definition: prolonged from a primary κάρ (Latin cor, "heart"); the heart
    • Key occurrences: Mat 5:8; 6:21; 9:4; 13:15; 15:8,18; 22:37; Mrk 2:6,8; 3:5; 7:6; 12:30,33; Luk 1:17,51; 2:19,35,51; 8:12,15; 10:27; 24:25,32; Jhn 12:40; 14:1,27; Acts 2:26,37; 5:3-4; 7:23,51,54; 8:21-22; 13:22; 15:9; 16:14; 28:27; Rom 1:21,24; 2:5,15,29; 5:5; 6:17; 8:27; 10:1,6,8-10; 2 Cor 1:22; 3:2-3,15; 4:6; Gal 4:6; Eph 1:18; 4:18; 1 Tim 1:5; Heb 3:8,12; 8:10; 10:16,22; 1 Jn 3:20; Rev 2:23

    G4151: πνεῦμα (pneuma) -- spirit¶

    • Transliteration: pneûma
    • Pronunciation: pnyoo-mah
    • Part of Speech: neuter noun (n)
    • BLB Count: 385
    • Definition: from; a current of air, i.e. breath (blast) or a breeze; by extension spirit
    • Key occurrences: Mat 1:18,20; 3:11,16; 4:1; 10:20; 12:18,28,31-32; 28:19; Jhn 3:5-8,34; 4:23-24; 6:63; 7:39; 14:16-17,26; 15:26; 16:13; 20:22; Acts 1:5,8; 2:4,17-18,33,38; Rom 1:4; 2:29; 5:5; 7:6; 8:1-2,4-6,9-16,23,26-27; 1 Cor 2:10-14; 3:16; 6:19; 12:3-13; 2 Cor 1:22; 3:3,6,8,17-18; 5:5; Gal 3:2-3,14; 4:6; 5:5,16-18,22-25; Eph 1:13; 2:18,22; 3:5,16; 4:3-4,23,30; Heb 10:15

    G2537: καινός (kainos) -- new (in quality)¶

    • Transliteration: kainós
    • Pronunciation: kahee-nos
    • Part of Speech: adjective (adj)
    • BLB Count: 44
    • Definition: of uncertain affinity; new (especially in freshness)
    • Key occurrences: Mat 9:11; 13:52; 26:28-29; Mrk 1:27; 2:21-22; 14:24-25; 16:17; Luk 5:36,38; 22:20; Jhn 13:34; 19:41; 1 Cor 11:25; 2 Cor 3:6; 5:17; Gal 6:15; Eph 2:15; 4:24; Heb 8:8,13; 9:15; 2 Pet 3:13; Rev 2:17; 3:12; 5:9; 21:1-2,5

    G1271: διάνοια (dianoia) -- mind/understanding¶

    • Transliteration: diánoia
    • Pronunciation: dee-an-oy-ah
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 13
    • Definition: from and; deep thought, properly, the faculty (mind or its disposition)
    • Occurrences: Mat 22:37; Mrk 12:30; Luk 1:51; 10:27; Eph 1:18; 2:3; 4:18; Col 1:21; Heb 8:10; 10:16; 1 Pet 1:13; 2 Pet 3:1; 1 Jn 5:20

    G4560: σάρκινος (sarkinos) -- fleshy/made of flesh¶

    • Transliteration: sárkinos
    • Pronunciation: sar-kee-nos
    • Part of Speech: adjective (adj)
