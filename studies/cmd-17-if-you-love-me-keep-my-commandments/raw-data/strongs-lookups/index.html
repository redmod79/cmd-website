    Raw Strong's Lookups¶

    Hebrew¶

    H157 -- 'ahab (to love)¶

    • Transliteration: 'ahab
    • Pronunciation: aw-hab
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 208
    • Definition: a primitive root; to have affection for (sexually or otherwise): love, like, friend
    • 211 total KJV occurrences; top translations: loveth (14), loved (12), love (10), to love (8)
    • LXX maps: G25 agapao (144x, PMI 6.51), G5368 phileo (12x), G3404 miseo (24x -- antonym context), G5384 philos (11x)

    H160 -- 'ahabah (love, noun)¶

    • Transliteration: 'ahabah
    • Pronunciation: a-hab-aw
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 40
    • Definition: feminine of H157 and meaning the same: love
    • Key occurrences: Song of Songs (9x), Jeremiah 2:2, 31:3, Hosea 11:4

    H4687 -- mitsvah (commandment)¶

    • Transliteration: mitsvah
    • Pronunciation: mits-vaw
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 181
    • Definition: from H6680; a command, whether human or divine (collectively, the Law): commanded, commandment, law, ordinance, precept
    • 190 total KJV occurrences; top translations: the commandments (27), his commandments (24), my commandments (23), thy commandments (20)
    • LXX maps: G1785 entole (153x, PMI 6.75), G5442 phylasso (66x), G1345 dikaioma (27x), G1781 entellomai (46x)

    H8104 -- shamar (to keep, guard)¶

    • Transliteration: shamar
    • Pronunciation: shaw-mar
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 468
    • Definition: a primitive root; properly, to hedge about (as with thorns), i.e. guard; generally, to protect, attend to: beware, be circumspect, take heed, keep, mark, look narrowly, observe, preserve, regard, reserve, save, sure, watch
    • 508 total KJV occurrences; top translations: keep (29), to keep (24), kept (23), and keep (19)
    • LXX maps: G5442 phylasso (355x, PMI 5.55), G1785 entole (78x), G5438 phylake (36x), G1345 dikaioma (38x), G5083 tereo (10x, PMI 4.78)

    H8451 -- torah (law, instruction)¶

    • Transliteration: towrah
    • Pronunciation: to-raw
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 219
    • Definition: from H3384; a precept or statute, especially the Decalogue or Pentateuch: law

    H3820 -- leb (heart)¶

    • Transliteration: leb
    • Pronunciation: labe
    • POS: masculine noun
    • Definition: a form of H3824; the heart; also used figuratively: heart

    Greek¶

    G25 -- agapao (to love)¶

    • Transliteration: agapao
    • Pronunciation: ag-ap-ah
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 142
    • Definition: perhaps from agan (much); to love (in a social or moral sense): beloved, love. Compare G5368.
    • 73 total KJV occurrences; top translations: love (13), Thou shalt love (8), loveth (6), beloved (6)
    • Hebrew source via LXX: H157 'ahab (144x, PMI 6.51)

    G26 -- agape (love, noun)¶

    • Transliteration: agape
    • Pronunciation: ag-ah-pay
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 117
    • Definition: from G25; love, i.e. affection or benevolence; specially (plural) a love-feast
    • 84 total KJV occurrences; top translations: love (45), charity (21), of love (4)

    G1785 -- entole (commandment)¶

    • Transliteration: entole
    • Pronunciation: en-tol-ay
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 71
    • Definition: from G1781; injunction, i.e. an authoritative prescription: commandment, precept
    • 43 total KJV occurrences; top translations: commandment (23), commandments (12), precept (2)
    • Key NT verses: Mat 22:36,38,40; John 10:18; 12:49,50; 13:34; 14:15,21,31; 15:10,12; Rom 7:8-13; 13:9; 1 Cor 7:19; 1 John 2:3,7; 3:22-24; 4:21; 5:2,3; 2 John 1:4-6; Rev 12:17; 14:12; 22:14
    • Hebrew source: H4687 mitsvah (153x in LXX)

    G5083 -- tereo (to keep, guard)¶

    • Transliteration: tereo
    • Pronunciation: tay-reh-o
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 75
    • Definition: from teros (a watch; perhaps akin to G2334); to guard (from loss or injury): hold fast, keep, observe, preserve, reserve, watch
    • 47 total KJV occurrences; top translations: keep (7), to keep (4), observe (3), keepeth (3)
    • Key NT verses: John 14:15,21,23,24; 15:10,20; 17:6,11,15; 1 John 2:3,5; 3:22,24; 5:2,3,18; Rev 1:3; 2:26; 3:3,8,10; 12:17; 14:12; 16:15; 22:7,9
    • Hebrew source via LXX: H5341 natsar (8x, PMI 7.27), H8104 shamar (10x, PMI 4.78)

    G5442 -- phylasso (to guard, keep)¶

    • Transliteration: phylasso
    • Pronunciation: foo-las-so
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 30
    • Definition: probably from G5443 through the idea of isolation; to watch, i.e. be on guard; by implication, to preserve, obey, avoid: beware, keep (self), observe, save
    • 29 total KJV occurrences; top translations: keep (6), to keep (3), have I kept (2), beware (2)
    • Key NT verses: Mat 19:20; Mark 10:20; Luke 11:28; John 12:25,47; 17:12; Acts 7:53; 21:24; Rom 2:26; Gal 6:13; 1 John 5:21
    • Hebrew source: H8104 shamar (355x in LXX -- primary equivalent)

    G1437 -- ean (if, conditional particle)¶

    • Transliteration: ean
    • Pronunciation: eh-an
    • POS: conjunction
    • BLB Count: 276
    • Definition: from G1487 and G302; a conditional particle; in case that, provided
    • 217 total KJV occurrences; top translations: if (127), If (22), except (18), though (11)

    G3551 -- nomos (law)¶

    • Transliteration: nomos
    • Pronunciation: nom-os
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 197
    • Definition: from a primary nemo (to parcel out, especially food or grazing to animals); law

    G4137 -- pleroo (to make full, fulfill)¶

    • Transliteration: pleroo
    • Pronunciation: play-ro-o
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 90
    • Definition: from G4134; to make replete, i.e. to cram (a net), level up
    • Key verse: Gal 5:14 "all the law is fulfilled (peplerotai) in one word"

    G458 -- anomia (lawlessness)¶

    • Transliteration: anomia
    • Pronunciation: an-om-ee-ah
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 15
    • Definition: from G459; illegality, i.e. violation of law or (genitive case) wickedness: iniquity, transgression of the law, unrighteousness
    • Key verse: 1 John 3:4 "sin is the transgression of the law (anomia)"
